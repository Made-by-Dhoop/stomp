About STOMP

Stomp is an independent street culture store in the heart of Bournemouth's Arcade. We stock the brands people actually wear — Nike Air Max, Stone Island, Stüssy, Carhartt, North Face, football kits, graffiti supplies and more.

No algorithms. No drops you need to queue online for. Just good stock, fairly priced, in a shop you can walk into. Come have a look around.

Our stock changes too fast to keep a website up to date. Get in touch or visit us in Bournemouth, The Arcade.
